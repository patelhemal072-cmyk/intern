Pre-Production Operations (Strategic Planning Phase)

Foundation Stage: Strategic Clarity

Before any shoot or design execution begins, strategic clarity is essential. Proper planning eliminates production errors, reduces costs, and ensures goal-driven output.

Key Operations

Client requirement analysis (B2B vs B2C positioning differences)
Product or service positioning analysis
Competitor visual benchmarking
Shoot objective definition (Sales, Branding, Catalogue, Performance Ads, Corporate Presentation)
Detailed shot list planning
Angle planning (Front, 45°, Top view, Macro, Lifestyle framing, etc.)
Background selection (White, Textured, Studio setup, Real-life environment)
Lighting setup planning
Prop selection aligned with brand tone
Script planning (for video projects)
Moodboard creation for visual direction
Budget estimation and cost allocation
Timeline planning and milestone scheduling
Resource allocation (camera equipment, lighting systems, production team)

A structured Visual Production Plan that ensures clarity, efficiency, and performance alignment.
